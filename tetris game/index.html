Control

✔ Control on the computer, using the keyboard and touch gestures.

✔ Control on a smartphone or tablet, using buttons and touch gestures.

Keyboard

Use the arrows and the space bar on the keyboard. The up arrow is to twist the shape. Space bar - moves the shape down instantly. All the remaining arrows move the shape according to their directions.

Buttons

To move the shape, use the buttons according to their directions. Double arrow - move the shape down instantly.

Touch gestures

Use gestures to move the figure. Touch the screen to rotate the figure. Swipe down on the screen - moves the shape instantly down. All other movements of the figure are in accordance with the direction of movement of the finger across the screen.
